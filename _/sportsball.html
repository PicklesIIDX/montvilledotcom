Sportsball

A combination of soccer, jousting, and flying birds. A competitive local multiplayer game that's as much fun to watch as it is to play.

PROJECT INFO

ROLES Designer, Programmer, Artist
PLATFORMS Wii U
RELEASE DATE November 2014
START DATE January 2014
TEAM SIZE 4

KEY CONTRIBUTIONS

SOLD OVER 70,000 COPIES
Sold on the Wii U eShop through press outreach, exhibiting, and working with Nintendo.

BUILT COMPLETELY DETERMINISTIC REPLAY SYSTEM
Built in Unity with playback from recorded inputs; supports backwards compatibility

OPTIMIZED FOR 60FPS 1080P ON WII U
Featuring custom 2D collision and a tight art pipeline keeping assets under the 1GB ram limit

MANAGED HIGH LEVEL PLAYTESTING GROUP
Frequent playtests with novice and experienced players allowed me to balance out 16 unique birds.
